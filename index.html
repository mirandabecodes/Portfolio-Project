About Me

As a Pacific Northwesterner with experience living in four countries in the Middle East/Gulf region, I have always had a passion for learning about people and appreciating different cultures. Before transitioning into tech, I spent twelve years as an elementary school teacher, advocating for tech literacy and utilizing technology to enhance learning and problem-solving.

Once I started learning to code, I knew I had found my calling. The challenge, engagement, and potential to make a difference in a new way drew me in. As a lifelong learner, I am passionate about using my programming skills to help others and contribute to social justice, innovation, financial literacy, and overall human well-being.

In my free time, you can find me reading, hiking, traveling, doing yoga and lifting, or snuggling up with my rescued Saudi street cat Lola. I bring a strong work ethic and a friendly personality to any team, and I am excited to contribute my unique experiences and skills to the tech industry.
